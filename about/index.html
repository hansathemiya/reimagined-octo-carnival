About GSafe

Professional geological risk evaluations to protect homes, land, and communities across Sri Lanka.

Our Mission

GSafe exists to reduce landslide risk and improve land safety throughout Sri Lanka. We believe that everyone—homeowners, land buyers, and developers—deserves access to clear, science‑based geological assessments.

Our goal is to make professional risk evaluations both affordable and easy to understand, so you can make confident decisions about your property.

We focus on protecting families and communities by delivering practical, actionable insights—not just technical reports.

Our Story

GSafe was created in response to Sri Lanka’s growing vulnerability to landslides, driven by climate change, deforestation, and rapid hillside development.

Historically, professional geological risk assessments were either unavailable or too complex and expensive for ordinary citizens. We saw a critical gap—and a responsibility.

Since our founding, GSafe has been committed to bridging that gap with transparent communication, local expertise, and recommendations you can actually use.
